# ARIS Compensation Framework

## TSM Override Structure
- TSM earns overrides on all personally recruited and downline agent production
- Override percentages vary by product line and agent tenure
- Vesting begins accumulating from contract date
- Manager vesting is separate from agent vesting — track both

## Key Product Commission Tiers (Reference: ARIS_Comm_Schedule_by_Product_TSM_6.1.25)

| Product Category | Priority Level | Commission Driver |
|---|---|---|
| Medicare Advantage | High | PMPM + renewal + override |
| Final Expense | High | First year % + renewal |
| Indexed Annuity | Very High | Single premium % |
| Medicare Supplement | Medium | First year % + renewal |
| Dental/Vision/Hearing | Low | Add-on/cross-sell |
| Hospital Indemnity | Medium | Cross-sell to MA clients |

## TSM Lead Allowance Program 2025
- Lead allowance is calculated as a return % of team production
- Separate allowances by product category (Medicare vs. Life/FE vs. Annuity)
- Allowance resets monthly — unused dollars do NOT roll over
- TSM must hit minimum team production thresholds to qualify
- Track team production vs. threshold weekly to project monthly allowance

## AMP Program Credits
Eligible reimbursement categories:
- Direct mail leads
- Digital advertising (Facebook, Google)
- Lead list purchases
- CRM/technology tools
- Seminar/event expenses
- Educational materials
- Electronics (within annual cap)

AMP credits are earned per application submitted by product. Track credits vs. eligible expenses monthly.

## Month-End

- Submit AMP reimbursement claims with receipts
- Update vesting tracker
