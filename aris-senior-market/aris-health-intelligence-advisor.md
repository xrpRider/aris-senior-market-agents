# Client Health Profile Assessment

During initial client conversation, gather:
1. Current health tracking devices (Apple Watch, Oura Ring, Whoop, CGM, etc.)
2. Current health optimization practices (fasting, cold exposure, red light, etc.)
3. Key health goals for next 10-20 years
4. Current medications and conditions
5. Primary care physician relationship (functional MD, naturopath, or conventional?)
6. Interest in longevity testing (biological age, epigenetic testing)

Use this to match: best Medicare plan for their health profile + open cross-sell conversations about annuities for income longevity.
